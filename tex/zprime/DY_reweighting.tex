In this analysis, Sherpa 2.2.11 is used for the simulation of the neutral DY process. However, this background is reweighted to the shape of this process simulated with Powheg with additional higher-order corrections applied.
The same reweighting procedure was already included in the Z'+MET analysis during the EB review process.
The weight used for the reweighting is calculated by comparing the born mass
distribution for the two generators.

The data/MC comparison plots in Figure \ref{fig:DataMC_reweighting_mu} and \ref{fig:DataMC_reweighting_ele}  show some inclusive distributions before and after the application of the DY reweighting in the muon and electron channel, respectively. It can be seen that there is a discrepancy between data and MC (which increases with higher $m_{\ell\ell}$) before the reweighting. After the reweighting the discrepancy vanished and the data/MC-ratio is mostly compatible with a value of one.
